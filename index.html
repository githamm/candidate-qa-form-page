Denver Post Candidate Questionnaire

Denver City Council

In an effort to provide Denver voters with information about City Council candidates, The Denver Post is asking all candidates to complete this candidate questionnaire. The Post will publish candidates' responses on its website in coming weeks, as part of coverage that includes stories about each council district. It also will give reporters more insight into each campaign and the issues affecting the district.

Deadline: Please complete and submit the questionnaire, and upload a photo of the candidate for use in The Post's coverage, by Friday, March 20.

If you have general questions about the questionnaire, email Politics Editor Chuck Plunkett at cplunkett@denverpost.com. If you run into any technical problems completing the form, email Kevin Hamm at khamm@denverpost.com.
