Denver Post Candidate Questionnaire

Denver City Council

In an effort to provide Denver voters with information about City Council candidates, The Denver Post is asking all candidates to complete this candidate questionnaire.

If you have general questions about the questionnaire, email Poltics Editor Chuck Plunkett at cplunkett@denverpost.com. If you run into any technical problems completing the form, email Kevin Hamm at khamm@denverpost.com.
